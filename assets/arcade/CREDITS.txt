Inferno arcade assets
=====================

Gun sprites (assets/arcade/guns/*.png)
  Source: "LowRes FPS Gun Sprites" by Kiovenn
  https://opengameart.org/content/lowres-fps-gun-sprites
  Used under the license stated on OpenGameArt for that pack.
  Frames cropped for first-person viewmodels (pistol, shotgun, plasmagun, railgun→BFG).

All other Inferno art (walls, demons, HUD, particles) is original procedural code.
